The Image of the Day

Nasa posts an "Image of the Day" page every day. Here are 3 of my favourite images:

Nicole Mann

Nicole A. Mann being selected to become the first Native American astronaut from NASA. She was selected as a candidate in June 2013. On October 5, 2022, she launched to the International Space Station aboard the SpaceX Crew Dragon.

Cosmic Keyhole

The picture shown above is NGC 1999, a reflection nebula part of the Orion constellation. The light source at the center of the image is from a newborn star named V380 Orionis. It was shot using the NASA/ESA Hubble Space Telescope. 

Back to Top

United States at Night

This image is an observation from space using nighttime light data. It was produced with the collaboration of NASA and the National Park Service to better understand the impact of lighting on animal species in national parks.
